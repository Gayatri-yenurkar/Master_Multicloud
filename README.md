**# ☁️ Master Multicloud: The Infrastructure Architecture Guide**

Welcome to **Master Multicloud**, your complete Rosetta Stone for cloud infrastructure. 

In today's industry, companies are moving away from single-vendor lock-in. They need architects and engineers who can confidently design highly available systems across **AWS, Azure, GCP, and OCI**. However, learning four entirely different sets of proprietary naming conventions can be overwhelming.

This repository is built to translate those concepts, helping you design resilient multi-cloud architectures and crush your system design interviews.

---

## 🎯 What You Will Find Here
* **Direct Translations:** Head-to-head mappings of core infrastructure layers.
* **Interview Cheat Sheets:** High-impact definitions designed to explain *business value* and *blast radius* to hiring managers.
* **Architecture Scenarios:** Real-world problem-solving for cross-cloud latency, disaster recovery, and high availability.
* **Provider Deep Dives:** Isolated study guides for the quirks and strict boundaries of each individual cloud.
